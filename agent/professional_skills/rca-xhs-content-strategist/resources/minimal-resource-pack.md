# RCA Xiaohongshu Content Strategist Resource Pack

## Template

`mode_decision`: result, source-volume rationale, topic-span rationale, reader-task rationale, thematic units.

`series_note_brief`: note id, chapter id, working title, reader question, content scope, evidence anchors, estimated pages, previous/next bridge, no-repeat scope.

`single_note_handoff`: series context, three title options, page roles, core judgments, evidence/boundary language, visual action, transition, and publish-copy anchors.

## Example

A medical source spanning definition, diagnostic pathway, treatment choices, and follow-up is usually a series. Split by reader job rather than by equal page counts: establish the diagnostic frame, explain treatment decisions, then close with follow-up actions. Each note keeps its own evidence anchors and explicitly lists facts that later notes must not repeat.

## Checklist

- Mode follows full-source judgment, not filename heuristics.
- Every series note has one reader question and non-overlapping scope.
- Medical claims have evidence and safety boundaries.
- Estimated pages remain readable and never exceed 18.
- Titles are accurate, useful, and normally at most 20 Chinese characters.
- Series context reaches the current single-note plan.
- Rendering and validation code do not author content strategy.
